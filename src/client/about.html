About Us

In the United States, food insecurity affects 14.3 million people which is about 11.1 percent of the total population. As part of our small efforts, we present to you FoodShare. FoodShare is a web platform that tackles food insecurity by linking individuals to local food banks, surplus food from restaurants and grocery stores, and community gardens to help combat food waste and ensure those in need can find resources efficiently.

Here are our platform's key features:

  1. Food Resource Locator: Users can find nearby food banks, grocery stores offering surplus food, and community gardens using an intuitive map interface.
  2. Surplus Food Sharing Platform: Allows businesses to post available surplus food items, which users can claim, helping to reduce food waste.
  3. Community Engagement: A forum where users can share tips, recipes, and information about local food-related events, fostering a supportive community network.
  4. User Profiles and Alerts: Personalized profiles for users and businesses, including alerts and notifications about food availability or upcoming events.
